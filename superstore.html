Uber Fares Prediction

Background

Superstore Giant, an e-commerce stalwart specializing in premium office supplies, has expertly managed and delivered a staggering 9,994 orders over four fruitful years. Our wide-reaching presence across the United States and an impressive Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) of $2.30 million.

Problem Definition

Over the course of 4 years, Superstore has encountered a 6% decline in sales and an 11% reduction in profits

So, what should I do:

  • Identifying a rational root cause based on data.
  • Identifying average sales analysis (by state, sub-category product, and monthly breakdown for each year).
  • Conducting time series analysis.
  • Performing store analysis based on ship mode by region and state.
  • Evaluating customer analysis by state and segment sales.
  • Carrying out profit analysis.
  • Providing recommendations derived from market basket analysis.

Tools: tableau, python

Proposed Solution

  • Working with relatively clean data, thus eliminating the need for data cleaning.
  • Conducting consumer behavior analysis.
  • Analyzing product support, confidence, and lift values to determine the product with the highest lift value.
  • Providing recommendations based on the market basket analysis results.
  • Proceeding with further analysis using A/B Testing.

Tools: python, tableau
