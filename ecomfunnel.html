Uber Fares Prediction

Background

The company's core service is its ride-sharing platform, which allows passengers to connect with drivers using a smartphone app. Uber offers ride-hailing products to customers in their area Uber also offers other products such as Uber Eats, a food delivery service, and Uber Freight, a logistics platform for trucking companies.

Problem Definition

For 3 years, the fares for products have been increasing while the number of orders has been decreasing.

So, the method I use:

  • Identifying order interval patterns (season, time, day of the week)
  • Precisely finding the pickup location (data in the form of latitude and longitude)
  • Determining the root cause of a business problem based on data
  • Determining recommendations and their impact on the initial objectives

Tools: excel, python

Proposed Solution

  • Remove latitude and longitude that are not valid.
  • Remove the number of passengers with a value of 0.
  • Determine the pickup and drop-off locations, and calculate the distance traveled.
  • Determine the day and season based on the pickup time.
  • Predict the taxi fare per kilometer.
  • Analyze passenger behavior.

Tools: excel, python, tableau

Skill excel: data cleaning, filtering, formula
Skill python: cluster analysis, extract and manipulate data using python libraries, iteration and looping, problem-solving
Skill tableau: basic visualization, calculated field, interactive dashboard
